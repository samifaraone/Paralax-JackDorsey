Jack Dorsey

  • Jack Dorsey was born on November 19, 1976 in St. Louis Missouri.
  • He became very interested in cities and maps
  • He is a programmer and entrepreneur.
  • He is widely known for being the co-founder and CEO of twitter, which is a social media site.
  • Also he is the founder and CEO of square, which is a mobile payment company.
  • He went to New York University

    Twitter

    • Twitter is a social networking system created in March 2006 and launched July 2006.
    • It was created by Jack Dorsey, Evan Williams, Biz Stone, and Noah Glass.
    • In 2013 Twitter was one of the ten most visited websites and since 2015 Twitter has had more than 500 million users.
    • It has been described as "the SMS of the Internet".
    • Twitter got its name because "...we came across the word 'twitter', and it was just perfect. The definition was 'a short burst of inconsequential information,' and 'chirps from birds'. And that's exactly what the product was" according to Jack Dorsey.

    What is Square?

    Square, Inc. is a financial services, merchant services aggregator and mobile payment company based in San Francisco, California. The company markets several software and hardware payments products, including Square Register and Square Reader, and has expanded into small business services such as Square Capital, a financing program, and Square Payroll.
